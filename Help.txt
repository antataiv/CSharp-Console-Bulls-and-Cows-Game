"Bulls and cows" is game that is usually played with 4 digits,
but in this version of the game can also be played with 5 or 6.
The computer generates a random secret number with all different
digits. Then you have to try to guess the number. If the
matching digits are in their right positions, they are "bulls",
if in different positions, they are "cows". 

If you reveal the secret number you win the game.
There is 3 levels of difficulcy in this version of the game:
"Easy" with 10 valid inputs, "Medium" wtih 5 tries and "Hard"
with 3 valid tries.

Example:
Secret number: 4271
Opponent's try: 1234
Answer: 1 bull and 2 cows. (The bull is "2", the cows are "4"
and "1".)
